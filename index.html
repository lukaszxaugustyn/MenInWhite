About Us



Men In White is a property services company based in Warrington specializing in Painting & Decorating.
With over 15 years of experience we offer vast range of property services to residential commercial and industrial clients, offices and schools, both internal and external works at competitive rates and always with the highest quality of workmanship in mind. We believe in doing all we can to the best of our ability on behalf of the client and pride ourselves on customer service and attention to detail.

Our Services

Painting & Decorating

Are you looking for quality painting & decorating services? Look no further. Whether the walls look dull due to old paintwork or you are changing the theme of your building, when you need quality painting and decorating work, we can help. From paper and paint stripping to plastering, priming and more, we cover all aspects of interior and exterior decorating services. Our services will improve the aesthetics of your building and property. You will be able to increase the potential value of your property with various types of painting and decorating options. We will carry out fast and quality services that will suit your preferences.

Property Improvement

Although Men In White specialise in painting & decorating, we also offers a full range of property improvement and light building work services. We are able to undertake all aspects of home improvements including plumbing, plastering, studwork, flooring, tiling, kitchen and bathroom fitting, etc. We offer the same professional service regardless of the project’s size. It is important to us that we are able to work closely with our clients to ensure that the job runs smoothly and to our customer’s satisfaction. At Men In White we understand that keeping your house tidy and finishing on time is as important as finished job.

Property Maintenance

Whether you are a homeowner, estate agent or a property management firm, if you need maintenance services for your property, contact Men In White. We carry out top class maintenance services for all types and sizes of domestic and commercial properties. Our expertise in painting & decorating services will help you make your building look outstanding from inside and outside as well. No matter the size of your requirements, speak to us for a no-obligation quotation. At Men In White, we have experience of working on all aspects of property maintenance and building refurbishments.
